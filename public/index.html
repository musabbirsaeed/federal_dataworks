Workshop overview

Built for federal test, evaluation, and data communities.

DATAWorks is a collaboration with the Director of Operational Test & Evaluation, NASA, the Institute for Defense Analyses, and the ASA Section on Statistics in Defense and National Security. The event helps practitioners share applied problems, methods, and collaboration strategies that improve data-informed evaluation.

Improve T&E quality

Design of experiments, integrated testing, sequential testing, digital transformation, and human-system interaction.

Share methods

Bayesian methods, statistical modeling, data visualization, communication, and statistical engineering.

Advance emerging tech

Cybersecurity, autonomy, AI-enabled systems, software, space, hypersonics, and survivability.

Solve program challenges

Sustainment, data management, automation, reproducible research, modeling, simulation, reliability, and cost estimation.
